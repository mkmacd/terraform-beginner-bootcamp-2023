English Breakfast

Ah the very heart of England. The English Breakfast. No one fully agrees what has to be there to make it a full english, but the fuller the better. There's no fancy cooking. You just cook it all up and put it on a plate. This is my opinion. To be considered a full English you MUST have: Bacon, Sausage, Egg, Beans, Some savoury bread item (eg toast, fried bread). But then you can also include: Mushrooms, Hash Browns, Tomatoes, Black Pudding and bubble and squeak.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

As many as you want

instructions

step 1

Cook all the items, individually according to their cooking instructions.

step 2

Serve on a big plate.

step 3

That's it.
